Our Story

Where healing
found its home

Starla Schwartz, LMP · Founder & Spa Director

25 years of care
on Lake Bellevue

All Star Massage and Spa was founded on one belief: everyone deserves a place to truly heal, restore, and feel their very best. Since 1999, our team has brought that belief to life on the shores of Lake Bellevue — one session at a time.

"Whether you seek a program of medical healing, a day of indulgence, or both kinds of care, we are the only spa for you."

Starla Schwartz is a Licensed Massage Practitioner with a background in skincare, accredited at Bellevue Massage School. Her passion for making others look and feel their best led her to build a multi-therapist team of state-licensed professionals committed to whole-body wellness.

The team gives back to the community — donating regularly to women in need. Many of our therapists have been with us for over a decade, a testament to the culture Starla has built.
